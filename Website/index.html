FAQ

Straightforward facts about what Pocket Lawyer is—and is not.

Is Pocket Lawyer a law firm?

No. Pocket Lawyer is an informational tool. It does not provide legal advice, representation, or an attorney–client relationship. Always consult a qualified attorney for advice on your specific situation.

Will my data be used to train AI models?

See our Privacy Policy for how data is processed and stored. You can update marketing preferences anytime.

When will the app be available?

We are preparing for launch on iOS. Join the notify list to hear when Pocket Lawyer hits the App Store.

Can Pocket Lawyer represent me in court?

No. Pocket Lawyer helps you organize information and understand concepts. Court representation requires a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.
